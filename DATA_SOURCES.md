# Data Sources & Provenance

This project's models are built from **publicly available manufacturer datasheets** and **published measurement data**. Every steel record is traceable to its original source below. The underlying factual data (chemical compositions, laboratory measurements) belongs to its respective publishers; this repository redistributes only our own normalized compilation and derived features (see `data/LICENSE`). To obtain original source material, follow the links below.
